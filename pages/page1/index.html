Roblox is an online platform where users can create, share, and play games made by other users. It features a vast array of user-generated games and experiences, allowing players to socialize, explore, and engage in various virtual worlds.

User-Generated Games

Roblox allows users to create and share their own games using the Roblox Studio, making it a platform with millions of user-created experiences.

In-Game Economy

Roblox has its own virtual currency called Robux, which players can earn or purchase to buy in-game items, accessories, and more.

Social Interaction

Players can chat, join groups, and play games with friends, creating a strong social aspect within the platform.
